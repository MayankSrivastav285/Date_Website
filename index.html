I have tried so many times to explain what loving you feels like. The closest I can get is this — it feels like the first sip of something warm on a cold morning. It feels like exhaling. It feels like, oh, so this is what everyone was talking about.

You never asked me to be more, or less, or different. You just showed up — again and again — and somehow that was everything. I did not know love could be that simple and that enormous at the same time.

I think about the day I met you and I genuinely feel lucky. Not everyone gets a person like you. I somehow did. And I am not going to waste a single day of it. ♡
